ABOUT INSPIRIA'23

The most anticipated national flagship event held by IEDC MESCE each year is called Inspiria. Inspiria focuses on giving students the self-assurance they need to follow their aspirations and ignite their entrepreneurial spirit. This contains a series of occasions with motivational lecture sessions and technical workshops led by well-known individuals, subject matter experts, and influencers.
